Work delivery *

• Accurately scopes work and follows through on commitments

• Able to work without supervision

Availability *

• Punctual in showing up for work and all meetings (on-prem and remote)
• Mostly available when the team needs them
• Sends timely responses to emails and other communication channels
• Has a predictable daily routine with early warning for absence
• Gives reasonable insight into leaves and longer breaks
• In special circumstance when the team collectively decides to work outside of regular work hours, is flexible enough to be available

Communication *

• Listens respectfully and intently to gain full understanding of issues
• Comprehends written material
• Presents information in a clear and concise manner orally and in writing to ensure others understand their ideas
• Makes sure that everyone is aware of their progress
• Appropriately adapts the content, tone and styles of their message to accommodate a variety of audiences
• Participates in team meetings and asks relevant questions.

• Exhibits adequate knowledge of the tools/technologies to complete work

• Shows ability to look at situations from multiple perspectives (technology, business needs)

Quality of work *

• Work is mostly error free
